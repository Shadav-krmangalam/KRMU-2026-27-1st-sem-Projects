How We Prepare Our Signature Pasta

  1. Mix "00" flour with fresh eggs into a smooth dough.
  2. Rest the dough for 30 minutes at room temperature.
  3. Roll the dough thin using a pasta machine.
  4. Cut into the desired pasta shape.
  5. Cook in salted boiling water for 2–3 minutes and toss with sauce.

What Makes Our Ingredients Special

  • Extra virgin olive oil imported from Tuscany
  • Mozzarella made fresh in-house daily
  • No artificial preservatives, ever
