Our Story

How It Started

Bella Vista Trattoria was founded in 2004 by Marco and Elena Rossi, who brought their family recipes from Tuscany to open a small ten-table restaurant. What began as a neighborhood favorite has grown into one of the city's most-loved Italian dining spots — while still being run day-to-day by the Rossi family.

Our Journey

  1. 2004 — Bella Vista opens its doors with 10 tables.
  2. 2010 — We install our signature wood-fired pizza oven.
  3. 2015 — Expanded seating and opened our private dining room.
  4. 2020 — Launched our farm-to-table sourcing program.
  5. 2026 — Celebrating over 20 years of serving the community.

Our Values
